## XForecast Challenge 2026

The **XForecast Challenge** is a financial forecasting competition designed to advance research in AI-driven financial prediction. Participants will develop models that forecast future market outcomes using real-world financial data. The challenge aims to establish a common benchmark for financial forecasting and foster collaboration between academia and industry.
